Exploring New Frontiers in Urban Geoinformatics

Our research team has recently launched a new project focusing on the integration of AI-driven spatial analysis and participatory mapping to better understand urban mobility and accessibility. This initiative brings together researchers, students, and city planners to co-develop innovative tools for sustainable urban decision-making.
